How ExamX Works

1. Choose Your Exam: Select from JEE, NEET, IAT, or KEAM with comprehensive syllabus coverage.


2. Track Progress: Monitor your preparation across Theory, Practice, and Revision for each topic.


3. Study Timer: Built-in timer tracks your study sessions and maintains statistics.


4. Real-time Analytics: Get instant feedback on your progress with visual completion percentages.


5. Cloud Sync: Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud and synced across devices.


6. Smart Dashboard: Personalized dashboard shows your exam selection and overall progress.

Open Source & Contributions

PrepDyno is an open-source project built with ❤️ for students. We believe in the power of community-driven development to create better educational tools. Whether you're a developer, designer, educator, or student, your contributions can help thousands of students achieve their academic goals.

Ways to contribute: Bug reports, feature requests, code improvements, UI/UX enhancements, documentation, syllabus updates, or simply sharing feedback from your exam preparation journey.
