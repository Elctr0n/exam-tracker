JEE Advanced

▼

Joint Entrance Examination Advanced for IITs

JEE Advanced 2026: 288 days left
📄 Previous Year Papers

🎯 About JEE Advanced

JEE Advanced is the gateway to India's premier engineering institutes - the IITs. It's the second stage of the JEE examination process.

Eligibility: Top 2.5 lakh JEE Main qualifiers
Duration: 6 hours (2 papers of 3 hours each)
Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Seats: ~17,000 across all IITs
📚 Syllabus Highlights
Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism, Optics, Modern Physics
Chemistry: Physical, Organic, Inorganic Chemistry
Mathematics: Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry
💡 Success Tips
  • Focus on conceptual understanding over rote learning
  • Practice previous year questions extensively
  • Time management is crucial - practice with mock tests
  • Master problem-solving techniques for complex questions

NEET

▼

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for medical colleges

NEET-UG 2026: 274 days left
📄 Previous Year Papers

🎯 About NEET

NEET is the single entrance examination for admission to medical and dental colleges across India. It's your gateway to becoming a doctor.

Eligibility: 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
Duration: 3 hours 20 minutes
Questions: 180 questions (45 each subject)
Seats: ~90,000 MBBS seats nationwide
📚 Syllabus Highlights
Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics, Modern Physics, Electrodynamics
Chemistry: Physical, Organic, Inorganic Chemistry
Biology: Botany, Zoology, Human Physiology, Genetics, Ecology
💡 Success Tips
  • Biology carries maximum weightage - focus heavily on it
  • NCERT books are your best friends for NEET preparation
  • Practice diagrams and biological processes regularly
  • Solve previous year questions to understand patterns
🚀

IAT

▼

Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology Aptitude Test

IAT 2026: 259 days left
📄 Previous Year Papers

🎯 About IAT

IAT is the entrance exam for admission to Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) and IISERs. Perfect for space science enthusiasts!

Eligibility: 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Duration: 3 hours
Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology
Institutes: IIST + 7 IISERs
📚 Syllabus Highlights
Physics: Mechanics, Waves, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism, Modern Physics
Chemistry: Physical, Organic, Inorganic Chemistry
Mathematics: Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, Statistics
Biology: Cell Biology, Genetics, Ecology, Evolution (for some programs)
💡 Success Tips
  • Strong foundation in JEE Main syllabus is essential
  • Focus on application-based problems in science
  • Practice interdisciplinary questions
  • Space science knowledge gives an edge

UGEE

▼

Undergraduate Engineering Entrance Examination

UGEE 2026: 260 days left
⚠️ No Official PYQ Available

🎯 About UGEE

UGEE is a comprehensive undergraduate engineering entrance examination for various engineering colleges and specialized programs.

Eligibility: 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Duration: 3 hours
Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Research Aptitude
Focus: Engineering & Research Programs
📚 Syllabus Highlights
Mathematics: Calculus, Algebra, Coordinate Geometry, Probability
Physics: Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Modern Physics, Thermodynamics
Research Aptitude: Logical Reasoning, Data Analysis, Scientific Method
💡 Success Tips
  • Strong mathematical foundation is crucial
  • Develop analytical and logical thinking skills
  • Practice research methodology questions
  • Focus on problem-solving techniques
